Pita Pit Menu and Apartment Coupon

Project description

This project took place during my internship for The Pita Pit in Bowling Green. My boss needed a coupon with a menu on the back that broke away from the corporate standards a bit so that we could hand them out to college students and they would find the advertising different and appealing. We needed something that we could give to apartment complexes and landlords to put in their move in packets so that we could reestablish Pita Pit's college rush hours and regular traffic. I created graphics in Illustrator, edited the corporate photographs in Photoshop and brought all of the elements together in InDesign to create this front and back spread. After some constructive criticism from my boss and edits, this was the final product that went to print. We created hundreds of this coupon and it brought the traffic into the store as my boss had hoped.
